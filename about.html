Vitaliy Tsal

Who is he?

Vitaliy Tsal also known as Arthas (previously EvilArthas ), Papich or Hailrake. Popular Ukrainian streamer. Known for his scandalous and defiant behavior. On its stream collects a huge audience. Vitaliy Streaming not only Dota 2, but streaming in this discipline made him famous. Arthas also occasionally comments on some games at major Dota 2 tournaments.

Vitaliy's story begins in 2005 (at the time he was 15 years old), when he begins to actively play the DotA. By 2006, having filled a hand and having pumped a skill becomes one of the most famous "pubstormers" of that time. His builds on Windrunner with a quick Linken Sphere and Leoric with a quick Blink quickly become classics. Vitaliy also turns out to be a good poker player, and after school the winnings in online poker rooms brought him a steady income.

After he got famous people were paying him to stream Dota 2, it was in 2014. After that, he only continued to become more popular and capture the audience. Today Arthas one of the most popular streamers in Russian segment of Twitch. He has more than 580 000 subscribers on Twtich and almost 60 mln. views.
